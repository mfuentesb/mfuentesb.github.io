Part of the CANDOUR (Covid-19 vAccine prefereNce anD Opinion sURvey) project, led by the Duch lab at Nuffield College, University of Oxford. The study surveys public preferences for vaccine allocation priorities across 13 countries and finds broadly similar orderings across very different national contexts.
