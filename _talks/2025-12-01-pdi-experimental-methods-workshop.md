Workshop instructor, with Raymond Duch (Nuffield College, University of Oxford) and Mauricio López Tapia (Universidad de Santiago), for the intensive course *"Métodos Experimentales PDI – Universidad de Oxford,"* delivered 1–12 December 2025 at PDI's Escuela de Investigaciones Policiales. It was the first academic training activity under the cooperation agreement signed 25 September 2025 between PDI's Director General and the *Talking to Machines* Program (Nuffield College, University of Oxford), and was completed by 20 PDI officers, agents, professionals, and forensic experts.

Content covered causality, experimental design, hypothesis testing, statistics, external validity, and information experiments, taught using R and Python, culminating in a final project proposing an institutional response to a crime and public-safety challenge.
